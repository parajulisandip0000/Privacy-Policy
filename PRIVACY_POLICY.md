# Privacy Policy for Adverse Drug Reaction Reporting App

**Effective Date:** June 18, 2025

## 1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Adverse Drug Reaction Reporting application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

By using this App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in order to provide and improve our services:

### a. Personal Identification Information

**For All Users:**
- Name, email address, username, password (hashed)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Authentication tokens and session data

**For Patients:**
- Hospital number
- First name, last name, age, date of birth
- Sex (Male/Female), weight
- Contact number
- Ethnicity (Brahmin/Chhetri, Terai/Madhesi, Dalits, Newar, Janajati, Muslim, Other)
- Pregnancy status (Yes/No/Not Applicable)
- Prior counselling history (Yes/No)

**For Healthcare Professionals (Doctors & Drug Counsellors):**
- Phone number
- Designation, department, specialization
- Account status (active/inactive)

### b. Medical and Health Information

**Drug Reaction Reports:**
- Drug name and detailed reaction information
- Symptoms experienced
- Severity level (mild, moderate, severe)
- Treatment given and medication details
- Indications, dosage form, route of administration, daily dose
- Dates of medication start and stop
- Time after exposure to drug
- Hospitalization status, allergy card information
- Discontinuation status
- Outcomes (Fatal, Recovered, Recovering, Continuing, Unknown, Others)
- Images related to adverse reactions (optional)

**Drug Counselling Records:**
- Medical diagnosis
- Medical history, medication history, allergy history
- Lifestyle information (alcohol intake, smoking history)
- Prescribed medicines
- Counseling provided and average counseling time
- Patient address and department information

### c. Communication Data

- Chat messages exchanged between patients and drug counsellors
- Message attachments (images, documents, audio, video, other files)
- Message metadata (timestamps, read status, sender and receiver information)

### d. Technical and Usage Data

- Device information and push notification tokens
- IP address and device identifiers
- App usage patterns and interactions
- Activity logs for security and troubleshooting
- Failed login attempts and timestamps

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features:** Deliver core functionality including drug reaction reporting, counselling services, and patient-counsellor communication
- **Authenticate and authorize users:** Verify user identities and provide role-based access (Admin, Doctor, Drug Counsellor, Patient)
- **Facilitate communication between users:** Enable real-time chat messaging between patients and drug counsellors, including text and file attachments
- **Monitor and analyze usage:** Generate statistical reports on drug reactions, track app performance, and improve user experience
- **Ensure security and compliance:** Prevent unauthorized access, monitor failed login attempts, maintain audit trails for data modifications, and comply with healthcare regulations and legal obligations
- **Send notifications:** Deliver push notifications for important updates, messages, and case assignments
- **Maintain medical records:** Preserve continuity of care through comprehensive drug reaction and counselling records

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- **Password hashing and secure authentication methods:** Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing; JWT (JSON Web Token) based authentication for secure session management
- **Use of HTTPS and secure communication channels:** All data transmitted over secure HTTPS/TLS connections
- **Access controls and regular security assessments:** Role-based access control (RBAC) to limit data access based on user roles; monitoring of failed login attempts
- **Secure data storage:** MongoDB database with access controls and authentication; profile pictures and reaction images stored securely; chat attachments encrypted
- **Audit trails:** All data modifications tracked with timestamps and user attribution

### Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the services and fulfill legal obligations:

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while your account remains active
- **Medical records:** Drug reaction reports and counselling records retained for healthcare continuity and regulatory compliance
- **Communication history:** Chat messages retained to maintain records between patients and counsellors
- **Inactive accounts:** Data may be retained for medical and legal compliance purposes

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

### Within the App:
- **Authorized healthcare professionals involved in your care:** Drug counsellors can access assigned patient cases, drug reactions, and counselling records; doctors can view relevant patient information for medical decision-making
- **Administrators:** System administrators have access to manage users and oversee operations

### External Sharing:
- **Service providers who assist us in operating the App:** Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, database management) who are subject to strict confidentiality agreements
- **Law enforcement or regulatory authorities:** When required by law, court order, or government regulations
- **Emergency situations:** To prevent harm to you or others in medical emergencies

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access and obtain a copy of your personal data:** You can access your personal information through your account profile or request a copy by contacting us
- **Request correction or deletion of your personal data:** Update your profile information directly in the app or request corrections to inaccurate information; request deletion of your account and associated data (note: some medical records may be retained for legal and regulatory compliance)
- **Object to or restrict processing of your data:** Limit how we use your information for specific purposes
- **Withdraw consent at any time:** Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it by deleting your account (this may limit or prevent your ability to use the app)
- **Manage communication preferences:** Control push notification settings on your device and opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country of residence. By using this App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

- Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
- Sending a notification through the app
- Posting a notice on our website (if applicable)

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## 10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

- Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering the breach
- Inform relevant authorities as required by law
- Take immediate steps to mitigate the breach and prevent future occurrences
